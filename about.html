bolshefiction

Communism, as a Service

By Aidan Ewart

About

Bolshefiction is, essentialy, a text generator wired up to the Communist Manefesto. It uses a type of generator called a 'Markov Chain' and generates sentence-long slices of text similar to the Manifesto. As the Manefesto uses a lot of repeating terminology, this can lead to some interesting and funny text being produced.
